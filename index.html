📖 How to Play
Welcome to Tickle Fight!

Smashy the monster is marching toward your Cloud Castle! Your job is to stop him using the magic tiles on the board.

This is a memory matching game — flip tiles two at a time to find matching pairs and unlock powerful abilities!

🎯Match pairs to deal damage, push Smashy back, or defend your castle.
Every time you flip tiles (match or miss), Smashy gets a turn to advance!
Win by defeating Smashy or matching all pairs before he reaches the gates.
Flipping Tiles

Tap any face-down tile to flip it over and see what's underneath. Then tap a second tile.

✅Match! Both tiles show the same image — their power activates immediately. The tiles stay face-up and are removed from play.
❌No match. Both tiles flip back face-down, and Smashy takes his turn. Try to remember where they were!

Rocks dropped by Smashy block tiles — match a Glitter Hole pair and click either tile to remove them all instantly!

Tile Powers

Each tile does something when you match a pair. For example:

Feather Arrow — Deals 2 damage to Smashy

Every tile in your deck has a unique power — from pushing Smashy back, to peeking at hidden tiles, to blocking castle breaches. Check the panel below the board to see which tiles are in play!

Smashy's Turn

After every pair of tile flips — whether you matched or not — Smashy rolls a die and does one of these:

⬆️Charge 1–3 steps toward your castle along the path. The further he gets, the more urgent things become!
Drop a Rock on his current tile, blocking it. Match a Glitter Hole pair and click either tile to clear all rocks instantly.
↔️Swap! Smashy shuffles two nearby tiles — scrambling your memory. Watch out for this one!

If Smashy is Frozen , he skips his turn. If a Cloud Guard is on his next tile, he stops before entering.

Win & Lose
You win if Smashy's HP reaches 0, or if you successfully match all tile pairs on the board!
You lose if Smashy reaches the castle and you have no Cloud Wall shields left to block him.

On the Setup Screen you can customize your tile loadout and set Smashy's HP. Higher HP = tougher fight! The HP slider automatically caps at your maximum possible damage output.
