Tickle Fight!
A Memory Matching Adventure
Rulebook
1 Overview

Smashy the monster is marching towards the Cloud Kingdom! Players must use their memory and the magic tiles on the board to deal damage, push Smashy back, and defend the castle before he breaks through its walls.

This is a cooperative memory matching game for 1–4 players. Flip tiles two at a time to find matching pairs — each matched pair triggers a powerful ability. But every time you flip tiles, Smashy takes a turn and advances along the path!

Players
1–4 players, cooperative. Works great solo too!
Play Time
15–30 minutes depending on difficulty

3 Setup
  • 1 Choose a difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard. This determines which faces appear on Smashy's die.
  • 2 Select which tile pairs to include. The default setup uses all standard tiles for your chosen difficulty.
  • 3 Shuffle all selected tiles face-down and lay them out in the snake-path grid (5 columns, rows as needed).
  • 4 Place Smashy's token on the first tile of the path (bottom row, starting column).
  • 5 Place 2 Wall of Fluff tokens on the Castle board as starting defenses.
  • 6 Set Smashy's HP marker to the agreed starting value. We recommend 6 less than your maximum possible damage for a balanced challenge.
✦ ✦ ✦
4 How to Play

Players alternate between the Player's Turn and Smashy's Turn. The player always goes first.

The Player's Turn — Flip Two Tiles
Flip any face-down tile, then flip a second tile. Two outcomes are possible:

Match! Both tiles show the same image. Their power activates immediately, and the pair is removed from play (left face-up, out of the game).

No Match. Both tiles flip back face-down. Try to memorize where they were!
After Every Flip — Smashy Takes His Turn
Whether you matched or not, Smashy always rolls his die after you flip two tiles. See Section 6 for what each die face does.

5 Tile Powers

When you match a pair, the tile's power triggers immediately. Each tile type is described below with its default number of pairs in a standard game.

Rainbow Royals
1 pair (special)
Deals 9 damage to Smashy and pushes him back 6 spaces. This pair is a hidden cross-match — the King and Queen tiles must be matched with each other.
Cumulus Cannon
2 pairs
Deals 6 damage to Smashy. A reliable source of damage in your deck.
Feather Arrow
2 pairs
Deals 3 damage to Smashy. A quick, light hit.
Wind Blast
1 pair
Pushes Smashy back 2 spaces along the path. Great for buying time.
Unicorn Horn
1 pair
Pushes Smashy back 4 spaces. A powerful pushback when he gets close.
Rainbow Tornado
1 pair (hard/easy)
Pushes Smashy back 6 spaces. The strongest pushback tile in the deck.
Rain-Bow
1 pair (hard/easy)
Deals 9 damage to Smashy. Highest single-hit damage of any tile.
Freeze Wand
1 pair (hard/easy)
Freezes Smashy for one turn — he skips his next die roll entirely.
Cloud Guard
1 pair
After matching, place the Cloud Guard token on any matched tile. Smashy stops before entering that tile. The token is removed when he reaches it.
Wall of Fluff
1 pair
Adds 1 defense shield to the castle. Shields absorb one breach when Smashy reaches the end.
Star Wand
1 pair
Lets you peek at 4 face-down tiles of your choice without flipping them. Great for planning your next move.
Glitter Hole
1 pair
After matching, click either Glitter Hole tile at any time to remove all rock tokens from the board instantly.

6 Smashy's Turn

After every pair of tile flips, roll Smashy's die. The result depends on the chosen difficulty. If Smashy is Frozen , he skips his turn and the freeze is removed.

Die Face Effect Modes
Charge 1 Smashy moves forward 1 tile along the path. Easy Med Hard
Charge 2 Smashy moves forward 2 tiles. Easy Med Hard
Charge 3 Smashy moves forward 3 tiles. Med Hard
Charge 4 Smashy moves forward 4 tiles. Hard mode only! Hard
Rock Smashy drops a rock token on an unmatched tile ahead. That tile cannot be flipped until the rock is removed by a Glitter Hole. Max 2 rocks on the board at once — the oldest rock moves if a third would be placed. If no tiles are ahead, the rock hits the castle instead! Med Hard
Wall Smashy places (or moves) his wall token on the tile directly behind him. Pushback abilities cannot move Smashy past his own wall. Med
Swap Smashy swaps two nearby face-down tiles, scrambling your memory. Watch out! Med Hard
Taunt Smashy taunts the players and does nothing this turn. Enjoy the break! Easy
Smashy's Wall
There is only ever one wall on the board at a time. If Smashy rolls Wall again, his wall token moves to the new position. Smashy can place his wall on matched or unmatched tiles, but the tile beneath it cannot be flipped until the wall moves away.
Castle Breaches
Whenever Smashy reaches the end of the path (or a rock hits the castle), one Wall of Fluff shield is removed. If the castle has no shields left, the players lose immediately!
✦ ✦ ✦
7 Special Rules
Cloud Guard
After matching a Cloud Guard pair, immediately place the Cloud Guard token on any matched (face-up) tile on the board. When Smashy would move onto that tile, he stops on the tile before it instead, and the Cloud Guard token is removed.
Frozen
When the Freeze Wand pair is matched, place a Frozen marker on Smashy. On his very next turn, he skips his die roll entirely. Remove the Frozen marker after his skipped turn.
Glitter Hole
When the Glitter Hole pair is matched, the tiles remain active on the board. At any point on the player's turn (even before flipping), they may activate either Glitter Hole tile to remove all rock tokens from the board instantly. The Glitter Hole tiles are then removed from play.
Rainbow Royals (Cross-Match)
The Rainbow Royals are a special pair — the King tile and Queen tile look different but must be matched with each other. Flipping either one followed by the other counts as a match and triggers the full 9 damage + 6 pushback effect.
All Tiles Blocked
If every remaining face-down tile is covered by a rock or wall, the player cannot flip any tiles. Smashy automatically takes another turn until at least one tile becomes flippable again.
✦ ✦ ✦
8 Winning & Losing
Victory!
Players win if Smashy's HP reaches 0 from tile damage, or if all tile pairs are matched before Smashy breaks through the castle.
Defeated!
Players lose if Smashy reaches the castle and no Wall of Fluff shields remain to stop him.
✦ ✦ ✦
9 Difficulty Guide
Easy
Die: 2× Taunt, 2× Step 1, 2× Step 2. More pushback tiles, fewer rocks. Great for new players.
Medium
Die: Step 1, Step 2, Step 3, Rock, Wall, Swap. Balanced challenge with all mechanics in play.
Hard
Die: Step 1–4, Swap, Rock. No wall, no taunt — Smashy charges hard and drops rocks relentlessly.
